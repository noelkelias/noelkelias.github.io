About Me

I'm currently a senior at the University of Texas at Austin majoring in Computer Science and Math. I have broad interests in theoretical computer science including, but not limited to, cryptography and quantum computing. Currently, I am an undergraduate researcher at the LINC Lab and have the fortune of being advised by Dr. David Wu.

Previously, I worked at Apple as a software engineering intern. I was also a machine learning intern at Applied Research Laboratories and a software developer at the Thinkery Children's Museum.
