About Me

Hello! I'm a freshman at the University of Texas at Austin majoring in Computer Science and Math. I have broad interests in theoretical computer science including, but not limited to, complexity theory, privacy-preserving algorithms, and graph algorithms.

Previously, I worked as a software developer at the Thinkery Children's Museum where I collaborated with the Thinkery board to develop STEM applications for young students. I have also worked as a research intern at the Ellington Lab at UT Austin creating detection methods for harmful pathogens. In my free time, I enjoy reading, playing soccer, and tinkering with new ideas.
